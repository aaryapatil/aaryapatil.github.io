--- layout: archive title: "Outreach" permalink: /outreach/ author_profile: true --- It is when I am brainstorming and collaborating with fellow enthusiasts that I truly experience joy in science.

While I can see myself as a researcher working with transformational instruments like the Large Synoptic Survey Telescope (LSST) writing software, analysing data, and pushing the boundaries of what is known about the universe, I can also imagine bringing all that I learn back to India to foster the growing research community there.
