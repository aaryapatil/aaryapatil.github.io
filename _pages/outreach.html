Pan-African School for Emerging Astronomers

I am one of the organizers of the Pan-African School for Emerging Astronomers, PASEA, a program held every two years for students and scientists across Africa. I was one of three postgraduate stream instructors for PASEA 2022 held from October 5 - 15, 2022 in Livingstone, Zambia.
